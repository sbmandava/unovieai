The 'Worker Safety Guardian' system integrates AI vision cameras with YOLOv10 models in Industry 4.0 to provide a sophisticated worker safety monitoring solution. By learning from incidents, 'Safety Guardian' continuously improves its risk assessment capabilities, playing a vital role in reducing workplace accidents and promoting compliance with safety regulations in modern industrial environments.
