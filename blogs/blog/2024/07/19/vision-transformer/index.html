    Vision Transformer

    Vision AI and Impacts in HealthCare

    One of innovation with GPT is not only it's Natural Language Processing (NLP) capabilities which we are familar with CHATGPT, but it's vision inferencing capabilities of understanding the context of what's happening within picture frame of video or reading a PDF record of Bill and create a enriched context for a data pipeline.

    Vision GPT,is a type of pre-trained model that combines computer vision and natural language processing. It uses a transformer architecture to process both visual and textual data simultaneously, enabling it to understand the relationship between them.

    Key capabilities of Vision GPT Transformer include:

    Object Recognition: Vilt can identify objects in images by extracting features from the image using convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and then mapping these features into a shared representation space with text vectors through a transformer model.

    Semantic Understanding: By aligning visual and textual representations, Vilt learns to associate words or phrases with their corresponding visual concepts, allowing it to interpret descriptions of scenes or objects accurately.

    Contextual Reasoning: Vilt can reason about the context in which an object is present within a scene, understanding nuances that are often lost by purely computer vision models.

    Vision Transformer Capabilities

    Here's the simple version:

    • It looks at pictures: Like a normal computer vision system, it can recognize objects in images (like a car, or a tumor or doctor notes).
    • It reads what you write: Just like a language model, it understands the words you type or the random scribbles a healthcare team does.
    • It connects the two: The magic happens when it combines these two abilities. It can figure out what's happening in a picture based on what you describe, or it can describe what's in a picture using words.

    This capability has tremendous benefits in healthcare and medtech companies to optimize the data ingestions flows

    HealthCare UseCases.

    In HealthCare Payer and Provider Space, lot of pre-authorization and post-authorization or Billing disputs exchanged as PDF files

    Here is the example.

    Customer uploads a bunch of disputed bills to the EDS ingestion pipeline or SFTP server and as expected not all bills are OCR ready as you would expect, as they contain lot of hand written notes with dates and times written all of over.

    Doctor Notes Hand scribd

    What if the Vision transformer can read this bill and can answer the following questions.

    • What was the dispute about based on the written text
    • Who was the provider and the specific dates of service.
    • Are there any intresting asks and requests within the bills which needs to verified, like circles or highlighted amount

    Assuming GPT can do all this properly can it create a FYRE Compatible JSON Record with enriched context and hand-it over for further processing

    Risks

    Not all vision models are mature yet, unless they are specifically pre-trained on the domain specific datasets.

    Trust Issues

    Enterprises needs to be very carefull of not using Public OpenAI Models which have generic knowledge and perform extremely bad.

    Don't tie your systems to SaaS API providers Models who promise everything and says it's secret sauce (in reality they might be using Generic Models in backend). Ensure these models are deployed locally and you have complete control over version deployed and expected behavior.

    Summery : Vision GPT Use Case

    Scenario: A patient undergoes a complex surgery and receives a detailed medical bill from the hospital. This bill includes numerous line items for procedures, medications, and supplies, often accompanied by complex medical codes.

    The Challenge: Insurance payers typically have a team of specialists who manually review these bills, which can be a time-consuming and error-prone process.

    Vision GPT Solution

    Image Capture & Preprocessing: The hospital submits the medical bill as a scanned image or PDF. Vision GPT automatically extracts the relevant text from the bill, converting it into a structured format that's easily understandable by the system.

    Code & Description Matching: Vision GPT leverages its knowledge base of medical codes and procedures to match the line items on the bill with corresponding descriptions and standard billing practices.

    Policy & Coverage Analysis: Vision GPT accesses the patient's insurance policy information and compares it to the billed procedures. It identifies covered services, deductibles, co-pays, and any potential discrepancies.

    Automated Payment Recommendation: Based on its analysis, Vision GPT generates a recommendation for payment approval, outlining the amount to be paid and any necessary adjustments. This recommendation includes a detailed explanation of the reasoning behind the decision.

    Human Oversight & Review: While Vision GPT automates most of the process, human reviewers still have the option to review the system's recommendations and make final decisions. This ensures accuracy and allows for handling of complex or unusual cases.

    How it helps healthcare payers and approvers

    Faster Payment Processing: Automating the bill review process significantly reduces processing time, leading to quicker payments for healthcare providers.

    Reduced Errors: Vision GPT's accuracy in code matching and policy analysis minimizes human error, leading to more accurate payments and fewer disputes.

    Cost Savings: Automation reduces the need for manual labor, leading to significant cost savings for insurance payers.

    Improved Transparency: The detailed explanations generated by Vision GPT provide greater transparency to both healthcare providers and patients.
