We engineer AI that ships.

Unovie is an AI-engineering studio for Industry 4.0, founder-led by a team whose vision matches the most ambitious in the field — and measured by what reaches production. We take full responsibility for AI transformation — from architecture through POC and MVP to production readiness — on a foundation of frozen-base, self-improving edge systems that you own and can audit. While the market splits between multi-year transformation programs and big-vision manifestos, we take the third path: production-grade, agentic systems you own, delivered in weeks at a fixed cost.
